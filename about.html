About

What this site is, what it tracks, and why it exists.

The Manager Monitor tracks Chelsea FC in real time — so you do not have to.

The site provides a live, structured view of performance, spending, and decision-making at the club. It combines data tracking with satirical commentary, reflecting how fans experience the current era of Chelsea ownership.

What the site tracks

  • Manager performance, including results, wins, draws, losses, and form swings.
  • Match results and broader trends in performance.
  • Transfer spending, player turnover, and squad churn.
  • Success versus failure rate of signings and player trading outcomes.
  • Financial direction under current ownership, including debt, revenue signals, and spending narratives.
  • Club developments such as stadium matters and long-term projects.
  • Public narratives versus actual outcomes.

Update schedule

Matchdays are always covered. Beyond that, the site is updated throughout the week as new developments, results, and data points come in.

Tone

The tone is intentionally satirical, but the underlying tracking and data points are real. The aim is to make club developments easier to follow, easier to discuss, and easier to share.
